Exhibition.

Exposure is the annual graduate exhibition for the College of Creative Arts at Massey University. Each year the exhibitionâ€™s identity is refreshed. The 2016 identity is experimental, fresh and progressive. It reflects the creativity and innovation of the College, the students and the creative disciplines within.

Visually, the identity is about a student's transition from promising young talent to in-demand graduate, and is expressed through a dynamic visual identity and typographic system including custom kinetic type, bold colour palette, punchy patterns and logo.

The Exposure identity is a comprehensive and cohesive system that was designed to provide Massey with a framework that can be updated and revitalized in new and exciting ways as the College evolves in the future.
