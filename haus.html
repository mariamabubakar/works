Haus Property.

Haus Property Management has been managing residential property in Wellington for over 40 years. My task was to create a visual identity that spoke to Haus' Austrian heritage and values and their extensive experience. Market research revealed some of the challenges facing property owners. Our response was to tackle these challenges head on from the no-nonsense strapline ‘Property Managed Properly’ to the reductive brand conception.

The brand mark incorporates the peak of a house, and embodies the no-nonsense philosophy which sets Haus apart from their competition. The broader identity is brought to life through bespoke imagery and a clean, modern aplication – applied cohesively across stationery, marketing collateral, vehicles, office signage and website.
